After dueling multiple times recently, iv'e relized vampires are very powerful.

The combos that are possable to pull off, tend to be very devastating.

One combo I use would be cards("like call to the feast and ethereal absolution")that create tokens and power up.

So I created a high number of vampires with life link, and make them stronger to deal more damge then gain more life in return.

Of course that is my primary strategy, and it works very well beside my favorite card in that deck("GHOSTLY PRISON!!!!").

An opponent I faced of recent that used vampires, utilized life link in addition to A card called, epicure of blood for incredible life gain("and drain").

This combo effectivly drained my life clean, and added it on to his own life point total.

At this point you should also take into consideration, that I was using A mono black zombie deck("with a good number of zombies that have flying").

Holy crap right?

It was one seriously wild match, that resulted in my zombies being leeched to the point of defeat.

It felt like the damage I dealt was kind of pointless, due to the fact that he gained the life back.

But hey thats vampires for ya right?

And so it occured to me that vampires, are one of the more robust creature types in the game.
